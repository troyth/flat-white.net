Flat White is a bespoke ceramic and porcelain tile studio based in Brooklyn.

We transform off-the-shelf tiles with textures, treatments, symbols, and patterns that can cover walls or be combined with existing configurations. Each project is a unique exploration of color, composition, shape, and experimental techniques.
